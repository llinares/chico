About us

Know what happens behind Chico UI.

The project

MercadoLibre is a large platform with a bunch of needs. Every development and design team needed to be able to build quality and consistent web apps using an agile development process.

So we had to find a way to unify the process and guarantee design consistency and UX quality for every team. And that's why back in 2010 Chico UI was born.

The humans

Chico UI is currently developed and mantained by the front-end team at MercadoLibre. They're moving Chico UI forward, fixing bugs and improving the source code.
